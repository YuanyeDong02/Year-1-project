Why we build this webside

Many students around us are hesitant about what to eat every day, and this is a great problem for both new and experienced students, whether they want to try new restaurants or are unfamiliar with the campus and want to get acquainted with it as soon as possible, we provide a platform for you to post reviews of recommended restaurants and rate different restaurants so that you can experience new restaurants and integrate into the campus faster.

Page Description.

The original purpose of this webpage is to help students get familiar with and discover the restaurants around them and to bring you a better experience. This webpage divides the recommendations into three sections: food recommendations, drink recommendations, and supermarket recommendations, by clicking on them you can enter different categories, in which you can choose and browse your favorite restaurants, supermarkets, and by clicking on them you can see the details and user reviews
